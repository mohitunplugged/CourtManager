Late Policy

We are humane, but fair. If you are late, you get 1 game docked for every 12 minutes missed.

Scheduling Logic

The "First 4" Rule

Game 1 (7:30 PM) is locked to the first 4 checked-in players.

Queue & Penalties

Latecomers start at the back of the queue (Zero Rest Credit). You will likely wait 1 game before playing.

Streak Breaker Exception

The only exception: A latecomer can play immediately if it prevents someone else from playing 3 games in a row.

Quick Start Guide

1. Check-in (Before 7:30 PM)

Tap names on the Live Roster as people arrive.

Tip: Tap them in the exact order they arrive! The first 4 names you tap are automatically locked into Game 1.

2. Late Arrivals

If someone arrives at 7:50 PM:

  • Simply tap their name in the Roster to activate them.
  • The schedule for future games will instantly recalculate.
  • They will see a red badge (e.g. -1 Game) indicating their penalty.
